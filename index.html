What Is Paint 3D?

Features, Uses, and Complete Guide (2026)

Introduction

Paint 3D is a creative application developed to make digital drawing and 3D modeling simple and accessible for everyone. Unlike traditional design tools that often require advanced skills, Paint 3D allows users to create, edit, and transform both 2D and 3D content with ease.

In 2026, even though the software is no longer officially supported in some environments, it continues to be widely used by beginners, students, and hobbyists. Many users still prefer it because of its simple interface, lightweight performance, and ability to create engaging visual content without needing professional training.

What Is Paint 3D?

Paint 3D is a digital design tool that combines traditional drawing features with modern 3D modeling capabilities. It allows users to create images, design objects, and manipulate models in a three-dimensional space.

The software was designed to replace classic paint tools by introducing depth, perspective, and interactive design elements. Users can paint directly on 3D objects, apply textures, and rotate models to view them from different angles.

Its intuitive design makes it suitable for beginners while still offering enough functionality for creative experimentation.
